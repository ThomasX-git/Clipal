## v0.4.2

### Highlights (EN)

- Failover: improve provider switch logging (auth/billing/network/5xx/429) and include the next provider being tried.
- Logs: decode gzipped upstream error bodies so logs stay readable (avoid binary/garbled output).
- Stability: guard against panics in log hooks and desktop notification senders to prevent unexpected process exits.
- Docs: clarify that launchd `StandardErrorPath` is the place to look for crash/panic output on macOS.

### 亮点 (中文)

- Failover：完善 provider 切换日志（认证/计费/网络/5xx/429），并明确打印“将尝试的下一家 provider”。
- 日志：自动解码 gzip 压缩的上游错误响应，避免日志出现二进制/乱码。
- 稳定性：为日志 hook 与桌面通知 sender 增加 panic 保护，降低意外退出概率。
- 文档：补充 macOS launchd 场景下应重点查看 `StandardErrorPath`（stderr）以获取崩溃/panic 信息。
